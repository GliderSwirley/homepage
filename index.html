About Glider Swirley

We are a group of students (past and current) from Asia Pacific University of Technology and Innovation (APU), and South East Asia.

We mainly aim to seek fun, knowledge and experience while playing CTFs.

We formed "Glider Swirley" around November 11, 2012; a week before PoliCTF 2012.

We also started APU CSFC - Cyber Security and Forensic Club of APU.

Ever since, we have participated in a number of CTFs, our profile on CTFTime is at: https://ctftime.org/team/3132/.

We hang out at #apu-iac (APU CSFC channel) on irc.freenode.net.


Check out the writeups from our members via the "Blog" tab above.
